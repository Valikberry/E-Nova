What is E-Nova Energy?

E-Nova Energy is a trusted provider of renewable energy solutions in Nigeria, specializing in solar panels, power banks, batteries, and hybrid inverters. Our mission is to make clean, reliable, and affordable energy accessible to homes and businesses across the country.

Our Story

At E-Nova Energy, we believe in the power of sustainability. Our journey began with a vision to provide Nigerians with energy independence, reducing reliance on unstable grids and costly fuel-powered generators.

To achieve this, we have partnered with leading energy companies in Japan and China, making us one of the sole distributors of high-quality renewable energy solutions in Nigeria. Through these partnerships, we bring cutting-edge technology, efficient power storage, and sustainable energy solutions to homes, businesses, and industries across the country.
